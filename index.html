Visit A Coruña

Enjoy culture and sports in this vibrant district close to the center of A Coruña.

Top activities to do at A Coruña:

Torre de Hercules

Go inside the oldest Roman lighthouse still working in the entire world.

Castillo de San Antón

Have a visit at this magnificent castle, now turned into an archaeological museum.

Sea Port

Take a Walk through the galleries of A Coruña, and have a look at the sailboats tied up at the shore .

Town Hall

Located in Maria Pita's square, you can take a tour inside the city's Town Hall.

Maritime Promenade

Take a walk through the longest Europe's maritime promenade, with an astonishing length of 13 km.

Down Town

Being the oldest part of the city, get lost through its narrow streets, and stop by some restaurant to try some delicious local food.
